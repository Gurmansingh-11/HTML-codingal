Section 1

Science

Science is a systematic and logical process of inquiry that builds and organizes knowledge about the natural and social world, often using evidence and observation to test hypotheses and make predictions. It involves the pursuit of understanding the universe, including its phenomena and how they work. Science is a dynamic field, continually refining and expanding our knowledge
